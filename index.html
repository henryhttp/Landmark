Landmark is an iOS app that teaches people about the history of their surroundings.

Have you ever wondered what stories and events have shaped the world around you? Whether you're on the way to school or visiting a monument, Landmark allows you to appreciate the significance of the places you encounter every day. Landmark uses open-source tools to find interesting historical facts about specific locations around you, and displays the information in a shortform, readable manner.

Imagine walking down the street and suddenly discovering that you are standing on the site of a famous battle or the location of a historic event. By displaying historical data on an interactive map, Landmark allows you to explore the rich past of your community with just a few taps on your smartphone.

Landmark will published on Apple's TestFlight program in May of 2023. Can't wait? Come to exhibition and download a physical installation on your smartphone.
